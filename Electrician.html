Frequently Asked Questions

Is the AI better than a real dispatcher?
It's faster, cheaper, and never sleeps. While it lacks "human intuition," it follows your script perfectly 100% of the time. It won't forget to ask for the gate code, and it won't forget to quote the dispatch fee.
Can it handle complex electrical questions?
It handles the intake. If a customer asks complex code questions, the AI says: "That sounds like a specific question for our Master Electrician. Let me get him on the schedule to come out and take a look." This converts questions into paid service calls.
What happens after hours?
The AI works 24/7. It handles the 2 AM calls so you don't have to. It books routine jobs for the next morning or wakes you up ONLY if it meets your specific criteria (e.g., Hospital, Restaurant Down, Fire Hazard).
Can it book jobs in ServiceTitan?
Absolutely. We specialize in ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, and Jobber integrations. We read your live schedule and inject the job directly into the correct zone.
Does it sound robotic?
No. We use advanced neural voice technology. It includes natural pauses, "umms," and intonation. Most customers assume they are speaking to a polite office assistant.

No. RobotsHelper is month-to-month. You can cancel anytime if you feel it's not adding value (but once you see the booked revenue, you won't).
